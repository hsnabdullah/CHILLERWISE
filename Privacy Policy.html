At ChillerWise, we prioritize the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide to us when using our website and services.

We collect this information to deliver our services, personalize your experience, and communicate with you effectively.

The information we collect is used to optimize and enhance your experience with ChillerWise. We may use your data to provide you with tailored recommendations, improve our services, send important notifications, and offer relevant promotions or updates. Rest assured that we will always provide you with the option to opt-out of receiving such communications.

We employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Your data is protected using secure protocols and encryption technologies to ensure its confidentiality and integrity.
